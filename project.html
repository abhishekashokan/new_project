PLAY AND LEARN KIDS LEARNING APPLICATION

    Play and Learn is an innovative and engaging mobile application designed to enhance children's learning experiences through interactive play. This application aims to bridge the gap between education and entertainment, providing a fun and captivating platform for children to acquire knowledge and skills across various subjects.
    The Play and Learn application incorporate a wide range of educational games, activities, and interactive modules tailored to different age groups. By leveraging the power of gamification, the application promotes active learning, critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity in children. By combining education and entertainment, Play and Learn strives to inspire children's curiosity, foster a love for learning, and promote academic excellence. Through its user-friendly interface, interactive features, and comprehensive content, this application offers a powerful tool for children to acquire knowledge, develop skills, and unlock their full potential.

LIP READING USING MACHINE LEARNING AND NEURAL NETWORKS

    Lip reading is a skill that allows people to understand speech by observing the movements of the speaker's lips. It is a useful skill for people with hearing loss, as well as for people who need to communicate in noisy environments or with people who speak different languages. This project aims to develop a lip-reading system using machine learning which is designed to be helpful when say there is a situation where we need to extract conversations from recorded footages but audio data is not available or reliable, or a noisy environment, where audio/speech detection becomes unreliable and erroneous, also it will help to improve the quality of life of those people with hearing and speech impairments.
    The system will be trained on a dataset of lip movements labelled with the corresponding words and phrases. Once trained, the system will be able to recognize words and phrases from new lip movements. The system will be developed using a deep learning model. Deep learning models are a type of machine learning model that are particularly well-suited for tasks such as image recognition and speech recognition. In this context deep learning, image processing is used in lip reading systems because they are particularly well-suited for tasks such as feature extraction and pattern recognition.
